RIA's BAKERY

Da Bakery


Tradition since 2022

The Bakery was founded in Downtown Brooklyn by Ria Smith. Aiming to provide customers with an ideal spot to come and eat sweet treats with no hassle. Ria took it upon herself to get a team together that she trusted her and her friends and this way she could have realiable people that she trusted. It took alot of hard work and dedication but she made it happen. At Ria's Bakery N' Sweet Treats you can expect to find a calm establishment to relax and find whatever you may be craving. We offer quick grab and go bites, drinks, brunch and dessert.
